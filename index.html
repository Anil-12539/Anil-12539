About Hanalei Surf School

Our Story

Founded by pro surfer Evan Valiere in 2005, Hanalei Surf School is deeply rooted in the rich surfing culture of Kauai's North Shore. Our passion for the ocean and commitment to sharing the art of surfing sets us apart.

Our Mission

At Hanalei Surf School, our mission is to share the aloha spirit and the joy of surfing with people from all walks of life. We strive to create a safe, fun, and educational environment where everyone can experience the thrill of riding waves while fostering a deep respect for the ocean and Hawaiian culture.

Our Approach

We take a holistic approach to surf instruction, focusing not just on technique, but also on ocean safety, wave reading, and environmental awareness. Our lessons are tailored to each individual's needs and goals, ensuring that everyone progresses at their own pace and leaves with a sense of accomplishment.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert instructors with years of local experience
  • Small group sizes for personalized attention
  • State-of-the-art equipment suitable for all skill levels
  • Beautiful location in Hanalei Bay, known for its perfect waves
  • Emphasis on ocean safety and environmental stewardship

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We are dedicated to preserving the beauty of Hanalei Bay and the surrounding environment. Our school actively participates in beach clean-ups, uses eco-friendly surf wax and sunscreen, and educates our students about marine conservation. By choosing Hanalei Surf School, you're supporting a business that cares deeply about the local ecosystem.

The Transformative Power of Surfing

Cognitive Enhancement

Surfing improves neural plasticity, enhancing problem-solving skills and creativity. The constant need to read waves and make split-second decisions sharpens your cognitive abilities.

Cardiovascular Health

Paddling and riding waves provide an excellent full-body workout, improving heart health, endurance, and overall cardiovascular fitness.

Vitamin D Boost

Surfing exposes you to sunlight, helping your body produce vitamin D, which is crucial for bone health, immune function, and mood regulation.

Balance and Coordination

The act of surfing dramatically improves your balance, proprioception, and overall body coordination, skills that transfer to many other areas of life.

Mental Well-being

Surfing releases endorphins and increases the production of feel-good neurotransmitters, reducing stress, anxiety, and symptoms of depression.

Social Connection

The surfing community fosters a sense of belonging and camaraderie, providing opportunities for social interaction and building lasting friendships.

Experience the Holistic Benefits of Surfing

Surfing is more than just a sport - it's a lifestyle that can positively impact every aspect of your life. From physical fitness to mental clarity, and from environmental awareness to spiritual growth, surfing offers a unique path to overall well-being.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my first lesson?

Bring a swimsuit, towel, sunscreen, and a water bottle. We provide all necessary equipment.

What if I've never surfed before?

No problem! Our instructors are experienced and patient, and they'll make sure you feel comfortable and confident in the water.

What if the waves are too big?

Our instructors will assess the conditions and make a decision about whether it's safe to surf. If the waves are too big, we'll reschedule your lesson or offer an alternative activity.

Do I need to be in good physical shape to surf?

Surfing is a full-body workout, but you don't need to be an athlete to enjoy it. Our instructors will help you build strength and confidence at your own pace.
